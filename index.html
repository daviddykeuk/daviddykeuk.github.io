Career Profile

I am a highly motivated technology leader specialising in developing evolutionary architectures to help keep up with ever-changing business needs and technology. I aim to create development teams whose members are passionaite about the work they do and are to create the best possible products for thier users.

Architecture is fundamental when developing systems, however, the best architecture will be almost invisible to the developer, allowing them the freedom to create functionality without restrictions imposed on them by architectural overlords, but with the safety of knowing that their code is secure, scalable, maintainable and changeable.

As a technology leader, my role is just as hands-on as a developer, with the added responsibility of setting a vision and embarking on the journey to get there by coaching, mentoring and empowering the teams around me. Architecture, tools and processes should never be set in stone from the start, they should evolve as the system, developers, technology, users and business evolve around them.

This is where my skills lie, in creating this evolutionary architecture coupled with modern technology and tools and empowering those around me to utilise it for the best possible outcome. Whilst also rolling up my sleeves and putting fingers to keyboard to write tests, code and CI/CD pipelines
